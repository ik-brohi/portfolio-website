Placement Overview

A reflective overview of my practical experience in early childhood education and care settings.

About My Placement

As part of my Diploma of Early Childhood Education and Care, I completed over 280 hours of placement across two high-quality early childhood settings. My role included planning and implementing learning experiences, observing children’s development, and working collaboratively with lead educators.

  • ✔️ 2 placements across 0–5 year-old age groups
  • ✔️ Active contribution to daily routines, planning, and documentation
  • ✔️ Exposure to EYLF (Early Years Learning Framework) and NQS practices
1. Child-Centered Practice

Learned how to follow children’s interests, respond to their needs, and build trusting relationships.

2. Curriculum Planning

Gained confidence in using observations and intentional teaching strategies to plan play-based learning.

3. Professional Collaboration

Worked closely with experienced educators and families to support children’s holistic development.

Placement Reflection

This placement experience has significantly shaped my identity as an educator. I now feel more confident in my ability to connect with children, apply curriculum knowledge, and contribute meaningfully to early childhood environments.
