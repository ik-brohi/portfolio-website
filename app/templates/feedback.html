Feedback & Evaluation

I engage in critical reflection to evaluate my teaching practices and align with professional standards and the EYLF outcomes.

  • 🔄 Continuous cycle of planning, acting, and reviewing

Feedback is not just a tool — it's a conversation that shapes the future of our practice.
