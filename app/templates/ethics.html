Teaching Ethics

My commitment to ethical teaching is rooted in respect, equity, professionalism, and the rights of every child to learn, grow, and thrive.

Professional Integrity

I follow the Early Childhood Australia’s Code of Ethics and continuously reflect on my decisions to ensure fairness, transparency, and accountability in every interaction.

  • 🔹 Treating every child with dignity and equity
  • 🔹 Respecting confidentiality and cultural backgrounds
  • 🔹 Advocating for the rights and voices of children

Key Ethical Principles

Equity & Inclusion

Providing equal opportunities for learning and development for all children.

Respectful Dialogue

Engaging families, children, and colleagues with care and professionalism.

Confidentiality

Maintaining ethical handling of children’s information and family privacy.

Child-Centered Practice

Putting the wellbeing and best interests of children at the heart of decisions.

Reflective Practice

I engage in ongoing reflection to align my actions with ethical standards, and I regularly participate in professional development to stay informed and accountable as an educator.

  • 📘 Reviewing ethical dilemmas with mentors
  • 📚 Participating in code of ethics workshops
  • 🧭 Documenting ethical decision-making in practice

“Professionalism is not just about qualifications — it's about ethics, advocacy, and responsibility.”
